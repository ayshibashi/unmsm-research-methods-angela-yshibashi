## Overview

Develop a risk governance framework,  model or artifact for AI-based compliance systems that enables Peruvian financial institutions to identify, assess, and control the risks associated with AI applications while ensuring transparency, fairness, and justifiability.

## Paradigm Selected

## Justification

## Key Assumptions

- Design as an artifact or method, DESIGN SCIENCE implementation a model, if could an artifact to comply AI Regulatory Compliance Framework in the local Peruvian Financial System, including Central banking best practices and evolving legal internal and external landscape and propose the 7 foundational guidelines 
- Problem relevance The objective of this research is to develop technology-based solutions to address relevant, unsolved and important business problem that is constantly evolving and being criticized through worldwide many articles, for example the recently encyclical papal from Pope Leon XIV - Magnifica Humanitas – May 25 2026.
- Research evaluation Demonstrate utility, quality and efficacy of the model (or design artifact) for AI-based regulatory compliance framework.
- The path forward requires more than just better technology; it requires better governance. Only through comprehensive compliance framework can we harness the power of AI to build a robust reliable financial system and central bank, because that is not only more efficient but also demonstrably fair and accountable
